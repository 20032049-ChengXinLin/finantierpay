There are 2 different account types you can choose from.

Merchant - If you are from a merchant websites and planning to use the wallet to receive payment from the user. You should choose Merchant as your account type.
User - If you are planning to use your wallet to top-up and send payment to the merchant or another user. You should choose User as your account type.

User

You are required to set up your wallet pin for your wallet when you first login to your account after you verify your account through your email address.
You will be prompt to set up your wallet pin before you can view other pages.

You can have as many wallet as you want. Each time you create wallet, you must set up 6-digits wallet pin.

Go to View Wallets page and there will be button for you to create new wallets.

You only allow to enter numeric 6-digits with no space, word or characters. You are required to remember the 6-digits pin as you need to confirm the 6-digits pin before you can create new wallet.
You will not be able to create new wallet if the confirm 6-digits pin does not match with the 6-digits pin you intend to set up.

Go to View Wallets Click on update and reset pin button and reset your password.

Go to View Wallets Click on update and reset pin button and reset your password.

You will need your wallet pin when you made payments on E-Commerce Websites that uses FinantierPay as their payment method.
Your payment will not be process if your 6-digits wallet pin is wrong.

It could be following reason:
  • You do not have enough wallet money to made payment.
  • You have exceeded your payment limit per day.
  • Your wallet pin is wrong.

You will need to login to FinantierPay account and select wallet you would like to pay. If you have any voucher you would like to use to offset the payment, you will need to select voucher.

Please note:
  • Your voucher apply only when it is FinantierPay or the store Name for the Ecommerce websites that you are making payment with.
  • You will only proceed once you have select the wallet Id you would like to use.

You can top up your wallet on FinantierPay website or Payment Website.

For FinantierPay website, you will need to click on View Wallets and top-up button to top up your wallet amount.
For Payment Website, you can click on the button to top up your wallet on select wallet to pay page.

Right now, we only support paypal payment method. There will be more payment method coming soon!

You will need to select any of the button to top up your wallet or enter the wallet amount you would like to top up. You will be able to see the total amount you would like to top-up before you proceed with the payment page.
Please note: You will need to enter a minimum of $0.01 to top up your wallet.

You can transfer your wallet money to another wallet on FinantierPay website or Payment Website.

For FinantierPay website, you will need to click on View Wallets and transfer money button to transfer your wallet amount to another wallet.
For Payment Website, you can click on the button to transfer your wallet to another wallet on select wallet to pay page.

Please note:
  • You can only transfer money to another wallet when you have more than one wallet and the amount that you intend to transfer must be more than $0.00.
  • Your transfer payment will success only when you are within the payment Limit for your wallet per day.

Go to account Settings and click on change wallet limit per day to change your payment Limit.

Please Note: By default, your payment Limit for making payment, transfer money to another wallet and transfer money to another account is $1000 per day.

Please note:
  • You will need a valid QR code in order to process the payment.
  • You can scan the QR code only when it is a different user QR code.
  • The QR code you intend to transfer money to another account must have a valid wallet for you to send money to them.
  • You need to select wallet ID that you would like to transfer money to and the minimum for you to transfer is $0.01 and the maximum is your wallet amount.
  • The payment will not be process if you exceed your wallet payment limit per day.
  • The account that you are transferring to will receive the money on their first wallet ID. They can transfer money to another wallet if they do not want the amount to be in their first wallet.

For each cents you used FinantierPay Wallet to make payment , you will earn 1 points into your account. You will be able to redeem your points before the expiry date on Rewards page.

Please note: The expiry date of your points will be 6 months from the payment transaction date.

Your membership points will need to be more than the points in the rewards page in order to redeem voucher.

Please note: The expiry date of your voucher will be 6 months from the date you redeemed.

The user must hit a minimum of the points stated below for their membership levels to change.

Gold - More than or equal to 1000 points.
Silver - Between 500 to 999 Points
Bronze - Between 100 to 499 Points
Rookie - Between 0 to 99 Points

When the user first register an account, the user will become a Rookie membership. The user need to earn points by making payment using FinantierPay wallet so that their membership levels will increase.

The user will be entited to a cashback percent based on their membership levels each time they used FinantierPay wallet to make payment. The cashback amount will be deposited to their wallet ID that they have used to make payment.

Gold - 15% CashBack
Silver - 10% CashBack
Bronze - 5% CashBack
Rookie - 3% CashBack

You did not make payments for more than 9 months. Your membership levels will drop one level down from current membership levels and your points will be reset.
You did not make payments for more than 12 months. Your membership will drop 2 levels down from current membership levels and your points will be reset.

You will be able to view your points History and voucher for past 1 year from current date.
